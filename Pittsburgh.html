Row House Cinema

Row House Cinema

Ratings



Row House Cinema is a single screen theater in the historic Lawrenceville neighborhood of Pittsburgh. Each week they choose a new movie theme, which every movie is centered around. Their concession stand features natural popcorn with real butter, craft beer, local soft pretzels as well as vegan options. This is a perfect movie theater for locals looking for a new movie experience.


Location

4115 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Primanti Bros. Restaurant

Primanti Bros. Restaurant

Ratings


This restaurant has a very unique menu. Their sandwiches are made in a way that is different than others. All sandwiches have coleslaw and french fries along with the rest of the ingredients. This is a great place for locals to eat, especially the college students, as there are a couple of colleges within walking distance.
