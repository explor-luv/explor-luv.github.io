Acme Feed and Seed

Acme Feed and Seed

Ratings

Acme Feed and Seed is a local bar with a much different vibe from many Nashville locations. The size of Acme means you're virtually guaranteed to be able to find room for a group somewhere in the venue. The large ground level is nice for its mix of small bistro tables, bar seating, and sofas. The top floor is great for its views of Downtown and Riverfront when the weather is nice. On nice days, the rooftop can fill up quickly, especially as people leave nearby offices around 5:30 - 6:00 on weekdays. The drinks: a good selection of beers, many on tap, on lower levels, though they are limited on upper floors. The food: much stronger than you probably expect for a pub/honky tonk - the owner's experience in catering and high-end restaurants shines through. The service: attentive and helpful most of the time. As Acme gets more crowded, you will have to work harder to get a server or bartender's attention - much like any bar, however it is still worth the visit.

Location

101 Broadway, Nashville, TN 37201
